About Us

We are a brand new dance studio coming to Toronto, Ontario, July 2021. We offer Multiple styles of dance from ballet to hip hop and everything in between and we offer classes from ages 3 to adult. We have a Beautiful facility located on Queen Street Toronto, with three large dance studios, girl and boy changing rooms and two bathrooms. Our team is made up of 3 amazing teachers from all different backgrounds of dance background. Starting with Miss Baker, Miss Baker grew up dancing in all fields of dance from ballet to jazz to arco. Miss Baker got her Undergrad in Dance at York University. Miss Baker will be sharing her talents and be our Jazz and tap teacher! Next up we have Miss Jo. Miss jo has spent her whole live at the dance studio. from age 4 she was dancing she hasn’t stopped since. Miss Jo graduated from George Brown College and received her Ontario College Diploma in 2016. Since then, she has been teaching kids at different studios threw out the years. Miss Jo will be our arco and hip-hop teacher! lastly, we have Ms. Smith. Ms. Smith has been dancing since she was 14, once she stared you couldn’t get her out of the studio. Ms. Smith has traveled the world teaching and learning ballet in different countries. Ms. Smith Will be our ballet and lyrical teacher!

Services

Our dance team starts at age 3 and up. The dance Studio offers ballet, tap, jazz, acro, pointe, hip-hop, tumbling, lyrical, kinderdance, kinderacro, musical theatre and technique classes. Individuals will learn to respect and discipline through one-on-one attention geared to the needs of each student, with a major emphasis on fun! Whether recreational or competitive, the goal is personal growth and striving for excellence!
